How to cite this work
 
 
 

How to cite the Catalogue of Life on DVD:
Bisby FA, Roskov YR, Orrell TM, Nicolson D, Paglinawan LE, Bailly N, Kirk PM, Bourgoin T, Baillargeon G., eds (2010). Species 2000 & ITIS Catalogue of Life: 2010 Annual Checklist. DVD; Species 2000: Reading, UK.

How to cite individual databases included in this work, for example:
Yu D.S.K. (2010). Taxapad Ichneumonoidea (May 2009 version). In Species 2000 & ITIS Catalogue of Life: 2010 Annual Checklist (Bisby F.A., Roskov Y.R., Orrell T.M., Nicolson D., Paglinawan L.E., Bailly N., Kirk P.M., Bourgoin T., Baillargeon G., eds). DVD; Species 2000: Reading, UK.

How to cite the Catalogue of Life Taxonomic Classification:
Bisby FA, Roskov YR, Orrell TM, Nicolson D, Paglinawan LE, Bailly N, Kirk PM, Bourgoin T, Baillargeon G., eds (2010). Species 2000 & ITIS Catalogue of Life: 2010 Annual Checklist Taxonomic Classification. DVD; Species 2000: Reading, UK.

On-line version of the Annual Checklist:
http://www.catalogueoflife.org/annual-checklist/2010

How to cite the Catalogue of Life on the Web:
Bisby FA, Roskov YR, Orrell TM, Nicolson D, Paglinawan LE, Bailly N, Kirk PM, Bourgoin T, Baillargeon G., eds (2010). Species 2000 & ITIS Catalogue of Life: 2010 Annual Checklist. Digital resource at http://www.catalogueoflife.org/annual-checklist/2010. Species 2000: Reading, UK.
